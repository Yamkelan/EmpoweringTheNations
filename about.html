Our Story

Empowering The Nation was established in 2022 by Precious Radebe. The organization was created with a vision to uplift local communities by providing practical training and valuable skills.

Its primary focus is on empowering gardeners and domestic workers, giving them access to education and professional growth opportunities.

Our Purpose

Empowering The Nation aims to provide quality skills and training that help individuals secure better employment or start their own small businesses.

The organization believes in empowering people with knowledge and confidence to create sustainable livelihoods.

Our Training Programs

The organization offers two main learning opportunities designed to meet the needs of different learners:

  • Six-Month Learnership Program — A comprehensive program that combines theory and practical experience.
  • Six-Week Short Skills Program — Focused on essential job and life skills for quick professional growth.
